- NVIDIA’s 2023 financial report highlights 65% YoY growth in data center revenue, driven by AI demand.  
- AWS and Microsoft’s adoption of NVIDIA’s A100 and H100 GPUs in their cloud AI platforms.

EXPECTED IMPACT:
